Our Concept

A theatre of fire

Fire is at the heart of every course. From searing wagyu to instant smoke, each dish is finished in front of you — a quiet performance you can see, hear, and breathe in.

Tableside searing

Wagyu and seafood, blowtorched at your table — caramelised at the edge, rare at the heart.

Instant smoke

A glass dome lifted at the moment of serving — the aroma of cherry-wood smoke meets the dish.

Straw-flame finish

Bonito and select cuts kissed by a brief, fierce flame — a Japanese craft brought to your seat.

Our Wagyu

The 5th Generation
Wagyu Butcher

As Wagyu craftsmen, we raise our cattle in Miyazaki using our own original blended feed, carefully developed to create truly delicious red meat. We are committed to producing Miyazaki Wagyu — highly regarded around the world and often considered equal to the finest Kobe Beef — while continuing to pursue the ideal balance of flavor, tenderness, and rich umami.

One of the defining characteristics of Miyazaki Wagyu is the natural sweetness and rich aroma of the meat itself. For guests who truly appreciate authentic Wagyu and want to fully enjoy the texture, flavor, and character of the meat, we warmly invite you to experience it for yourself.
